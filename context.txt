General health tips:
- Fever, headache, sore throat, and fatigue are common signs of viral infections.
- Stay hydrated, rest well, and monitor your temperature during mild illness.
- For stomach pain, avoid oily/spicy food and stay hydrated. Seek medical advice if persistent.
- Antibiotics are used for bacterial infections, not viral infections.
- COVID-19 symptoms include cough, fever, shortness of breath, and loss of taste or smell.
- Diabetes involves high blood sugar. Controlled with medication, exercise, and a healthy diet.
- High blood pressure can be managed with less salt, more activity, and stress control.
- Always consult a licensed doctor for real medical conditions or emergencies.
